Bio

Thabo Sekgotho is an AWS cloud computing student at ALX Africa, a subsidiary of the African Leadership Group. He is an avid tech enthusiast and a budding entrepreneur, driven by the desire to make a lasting impact in the lives of those around him. Before pursuing a career in the cloud, he obtained his undergraduate degree in Information Systems and Computer Science. Beyond his academic achievements, he has also represented South Africa at the People to People Leadership Summit hosted by Harvard University and also worked as a transformation representation under the Equity and Institutional Culture office - an office dedicated to fostering a culture of inclusivity, understanding, and cooperation within the university community.
